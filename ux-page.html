Motto

TradeBox is a fully Responsive online mobile trading service for collector and sellers of all kinds. TradeBox motto is to make trading 100% safe, 100%reliable, and 100% transparent. Stealing and Scamming is common in the trading word, an easier online, so finding trusting trading service isn’t easy, but with our 3-step verification process it makes online trading the safest thing around.

Facial Recognition

before you can start creating a profile and organizing your trading items you must give us permission to access to your apps, camera, and search browser, then verify your identity by sending in a photo of your I.D and snaping a clear image of yourself on the app, with our facial recognition system.

Then we will get back with you within 24 hours with clearance to move further into the app or a IP banned on your device, if you feel our system was at fault we will give them the option to send us a email within 24 hours or the IP banned will be permanent.

Creating an Account

Next, you will be prompt with creating an account, after you place your information in you can select if you want your real name or email to be displayed or hidden, and no matter what your street address will only be shown between you and who you are trading with.

After you have created a profile you must verify your account in order to start trading, by linking two alternant social media pages, your email address, and phone number.

We don’t want user to feel safe, but be safe. Everything on the site pushes user to be as transparent as possible. We recommend to only trade with people who have a pinned video of themselves stating their user name and saying the date the video was made. This transparency is important in letting others know you can be trusted as well.

Listings

when listing items on our app you can post images or videos of the items, we recommend all listing include some kind of indication of your username and the date posted in the image or video, this also show 100% transparency.(

Messages

Messaging is like any other app, but we block certain key words and lettering, such as addresses, phone number, meet ups, and social media handles. We do this to keep all user safe and keep messaging within our app.

Conformations

Trade conformation will show up in messenger once both user come to an agreement within the messages area of the user being messaged. A button called “Lock in!” will pop up, that allows the user to confirm if the trade is accurate, once its locked in the other user must confirm if the lock in is correct. Remember 100% transparency.

Shipping

When the “Lock in!” has been confirmed from both user shipping QR codes will be sent to each user. We do allow you to print your own label if you wish, and once the first person has shipped off their item the other user has 24 hours to ship their item off or the trade will be canceled.

Fraud

if the item you received is not the same item, please notify us through the app asap. All recordings, images, messages, even deleted we have access to from our archive system that backs up all user information in the case of thief and scamming We won’t be able to get you another item. But will provide information for legal reasonings, if needed.

Vaulted Trade

if you want the safest way to trade, signing up for Vaulted Trades, which is a 16 dollar charge from both user, which has both users shipping the packages to our factory to inspect if the items are both what they claim, if process is clear we send items to the respected user. This is recommend for all trades on TradeBox.
