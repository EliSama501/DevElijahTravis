Website

The Website consist of a linear gradient background to give a fullness to the homepage, and the overall design of the website when traveling through the navigation/pages. To make everything on the homepage pop I gave it a Subtle border, blur, or halo around the imagery to give it that floating effect you see. the same goes for the button, even though we know most user understand what’s clickable and not we as developers and designer still understand we must help user navigate through the website or application.

Artboard & Color Scheme

The artboard was created to help me understand how I wanted to layer out things through my mockups to bring to life in the website and application. To me, as a designer a artboard is just as important as the color scheme, which is why I added the color scheme to the artboard to show the contrast of importance, and how well each color balances off each other, and that I understand how eyes view colors on portable-media and how it should always be soft and not harsh

Mobile

Cost-Free is about buying medicine at the lowest price valuable on the market, which is near cost, giving the name Cost-Free. When ordering medicine you’ll be greeted with the about, dosages, cost, and a add to cart button, and as you scroll you see more. Bigger is better, easier to see and click. So I made the quantity images you can click, and the image changes color as well as it convert to your cart at the same time. To remove just clock the image again making it easier for people of all ages. The Interface of a website or application shouldn’t just be pretty, but easy to navigate as well.
